Right now, **if you only run the project on your computer**, your friend **cannot access it using a link**. The Streamlit app runs only on your local machine.

When you run:

```bash
streamlit run app.py
```

the link you see will look like:

```text
http://localhost:8501
```

or

```text
http://127.0.0.1:8501
```

`localhost` means **only your computer can open it**, so sending that link to your friend **will not work**.

---

# ✅ How to share it with your friend

You must **deploy the project online**. The easiest way is using **Streamlit Community Cloud**.

### Steps

1️⃣ Upload your project to **GitHub**
(you already did this).

2️⃣ Go to
[https://share.streamlit.io](https://share.streamlit.io)

3️⃣ Sign in with GitHub.

4️⃣ Click **New App**.

5️⃣ Select:

* your repository
* branch: `main`
* file: `app.py`

6️⃣ Click **Deploy**.

---

# 🌐 After deployment

You will get a link like:

```text
https://smart-attendance-system.streamlit.app
```

Now you can send that **link to your friend**, and they can run the project in their browser.
